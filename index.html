What is Breath to Being?

It's not just an Instagram page, but a passion project dedicated to the art of being. In a world that never stops, we invite you to pause, focus inward, and shift from merely surviving to truly living.

Through mindful yoga asanas and targeted Pranayama (breathwork), we help you fade out the external noise. This isn't just about yoga; it's about exploring your whole, authentic self—celebrating every facet of a busy, beautiful life.

Breathe to live, move to feel, and be to grow.

Our Philosophy

We believe wellness isn't an optional add-on, but a way of being. We address the disconnects of modern life by fostering a genuine connection with oneself.

The Disconnect

  • • Forgetting to pause and feeling disconnected from ourselves.
  • • Experiencing high levels of stress, anxiety, and burnout.
  • • Treating wellness as a chore, not a joyful part of life.
  • • Believing a yogic lifestyle is restrictive or boring.

The Reconnection

  • ✓ Reconnect to yourself through yoga, pranayama, and mindfulness.
  • ✓ Find calm and genuine enjoyment in your wellness journey.
  • ✓ Learn to savor the simple joys and little things in life.
  • ✓ Discover that mindful living is vibrant, creative, and freeing.

My Story

Born in a small town surrounded by nature, I moved to the big city with big dreams. The fast-paced corporate life brought success, but left me feeling like something was missing. Yoga, which started as a physical challenge to hold a headstand, quickly became a profound connection to something deeper.

Overwhelmed by corporate pressures, I took a pause—a month in Rishikesh to immerse myself in yoga. That experience changed everything. It wasn't just a workout; it was a way of being. I learned to weave those lessons into my daily life, and that's how Breath to Being was born.

"This space is my digital diary. My hope is that as you follow along, something here may also shift in you. Because if there’s one thing my journey has taught me, it’s this: all the answers of the universe lie within you."
